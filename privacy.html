Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

FairPage does not collect, transmit, or store any user data on external servers.

How FairPage works:

  • All dark pattern detection happens locally in your browser
  • The extension analyzes page structure and visual design on your device
  • Detection results are stored only in Chrome's local storage on your machine

What we do NOT do:

  • We do not collect your browsing history
  • We do not transmit any page content to external servers
  • We do not run analytics or tracking code in the extension
  • We do not require an account, email, or any personal information
  • We do not use cookies or third-party analytics SDKs
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize any user data

Data storage:

All detection history and settings are stored locally using Chrome's built-in storage APIs and IndexedDB. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time through the extension settings.

Open source:

Our detection rules are publicly available on GitHub. You can inspect exactly what the extension does.
