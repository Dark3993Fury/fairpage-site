What we detect

We've categorized the web's most manipulative practices so you can browse safely.

Fake Urgency

Countdown timers that reset or are completely artificial.

Hidden Fees

Charges injected late in checkout after you're committed.

Confirmshaming

"No thanks, I prefer to waste money" style dismiss buttons.

Pre-checked Boxes

Add-ons and marketing opt-ins you never asked for.

Fake Scarcity

"Only 2 left!" with no basis in reality.

Hard-to-Close Modals

Tiny, hidden, or low-contrast close buttons.

Misleading Buttons

Safe choice greyed out, expensive choice highlighted.

Sneaky Cart Adds

Items added to your basket without your action.

Subscription Traps

Auto-renewal buried in fine print.

Forced Action

Demanding signup or downloads for basic actions.
